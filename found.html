Found

Lost & Found Tracker

Found is a centralized lost and found system designed specifically for hotels. It solves the problem of manual item tracking and poor guest experiences by allowing hotel staff to log found items, and customers to search for and claim their lost belongings. Features like secure pickup, optional delivery, and real-time updates make Found a powerful tool to increase guest satisfaction and streamline internal operations.

Showcase Demo: Found Overview

This presentation outlines the real-world problem behind lost and found management in the hotel industry, introduces Found as the solution, and walks through how users interact with the system.
